Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect your email address and WhatsApp phone number solely for the purpose of linking your accounts and sending you notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We request access to your Gmail account (read-only) to detect new emails and forward their contents to your registered WhatsApp number.

3. Data Storage

We do not store your email content in our database. Email contents are processed in memory and immediately forwarded to WhatsApp. We only store your email address, WhatsApp number, and authentication tokens provided by Google.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google's official API for Gmail access and Meta's official API for WhatsApp messaging.
